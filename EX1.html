About Us

Koski Homes is a leading property management and construction firm dedicated to helping homeowners, investors, and developers build and manage properties with confidence. We manage tenant payments, maintain tenant records, and assist those eager to start their apartment projects. Our approach combines professionalism with the warmth of home, serving both local and international clients — especially Kenyans abroad looking to reinvest back home.
